Interplanetary Banhammer

Who are we?

We are a group of large servers united with one common goal: keeping our servers clean. We decided to put our efforts towards this goal by sharing the IDs of Discord users who take away from our community more than they give. This includes trolls, spammers, serial shitposters, raiders, and others along this line. They are not productive members of the community, and therefore, they should be excluded.
For this reason, we've created our own ban network of sorts, where if a user is banned in one partnered server, it may also lead to a ban in one of the other partnered servers.

What we do

Let's say for example User Spammy McSpamface joins r/Apple and immediately starts posting racist memes in the chat. Obviously, they've made some poor life choices that would lead them to do this. Regardless, they're clearly not productive members of the communtiy. A moderator will ban them, and then take their ID and report it on the Interplanetary Banhammer server in the reports channel. Partnered servers can now decide amongst themselves whether or not they feel Spammy's actions are deserving of a ban in their own servers, and if so, they will ban them.
