Who are we?

We're a group of large affiliated servers with one common goal: keeping our servers clean. We decided to put our efforts towards this goal by sharing the IDs of Discord users who take away from our community more than they give. This includes trolls, spammers, serial shitposters, raiders, and others along this line. They are not productive members of the community, and therefore, they should be excluded.
